ochazuke

Enjoy the wonderful world of ochazuke!

A simple Japanese dish made by pouring green tea, dashi, or hot water over cooked rice. Common toppings include tsukemono (pickled vegetables), umeboshi (pickled ume), nori (seaweed), furikake, sesame seeds and tarako (salted and marinated pollock roe), salted salmon, shiokara (pickled seafood), scallions, and wasabi.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Broiled Salmon
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Green Tea

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients
  2. Broil Salmon
  3. Arrange ingredients in bowl
  4. Pour in green tea
